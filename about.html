About

Goal

The primary goal of the Application Development Initiative is to nurture student creativity and technological aptitude by creating a communal resource for developers of all talents and interests.

Get Involved

Columbia University students of all backgrounds are invited and encouraged to take an active role in this initiative.

You can get involved by subscribing to our mailing list and coming to our events. We hope to see you around!
